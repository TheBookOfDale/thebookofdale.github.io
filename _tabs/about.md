## Who I Am

I’m Christopher Dale — a Data & Analytics leader focused on building teams that operate with clarity, deliver trusted insights, and scale through strong systems and processes.  
My work blends **Process‑First Data Strategy**, **operating‑model clarity**, and **decision intelligence** to help organizations make better decisions, faster and more reliably.

I believe analytics succeeds when teams understand their purpose, their operating model, and the decisions they exist to support.

---

## What I Do

I lead analytics and operational systems teams that sit at the intersection of data, process, and technology.  
My work centers on:

- designing and stewarding business‑critical systems  
- building analytics workflows that scale  
- improving decision‑making through better models and processes  
- enabling teams with clear structures, expectations, and feedback loops  

I focus on creating environments where analysts, engineers, and operators can do their best work — with clarity, confidence, and alignment.

---

## How I Lead

My leadership philosophy is simple:

- **Clarity creates speed**  
- **Structure creates trust**  
- **Systems create scale**

I build teams around clear decision rights, predictable intake, strong governance, and a shared understanding of how analytics supports the business.  
I emphasize communication, enablement, and continuous improvement — because high‑functioning teams don’t happen by accident.
